FASHIAN

Nestled in the heart of Hartenstraat, Fashian stands out as a second-hand designer haven, possibly making a cameo in one of Emma Chamberlain's vlogs.

Ian, the discerning owner, showcases an exceptional knack for curating unique pieces, evident in the store's expansive array of distinctive vintage and high-end items.

LOCATION: Hartenstraat 1, 1016 BZ Amsterdam
