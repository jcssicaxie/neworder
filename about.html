The New Order gives light to high-end fashion pieces that are still alive by today and craving to be owned.

From timeless designer pieces like, Prada, Rick Owens and Louis Vuitton the collections ranges to Maison Margiela, Thierry Mugler and Issey Miyake.

This platform is founded with the purpose to highlight sustainable vintage shops that are in scarce category, located in city Amsterdam.
