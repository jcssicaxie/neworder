Amsterdam Vintage Clothing

Founded in January 2020 by Tim van Soest, Amsterdam Vintage Clothing (AVC) stands as a dedicated force in the world of fashion. This sustainable clothing retailer takes pride in its expertise in curating globally sourced vintage clothing and accessories from renowned designer brands.

Each item not only reflects a sense of individuality but also contributes to sustainable fashion by giving pre-loved products a new lease on life.

LOCATION: Prinsengracht 226, 1016 HD Amsterdam
