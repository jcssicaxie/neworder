Amsterdam Vintage Clothing

Founded in January 2020 by Tim van Soest, Amsterdam Vintage Clothing (AVC) stands as a conscientious force in the world of fashion. Operating both online and offline, this sustainable clothing retailer takes pride in its expertise in curating globally sourced vintage clothing and accessories from renowned designer brands.

When you choose to shop at Amsterdam Vintage Clothing, you embark on a unique journey to discover distinctive pieces that set you apart. Each item not only reflects a sense of individuality but also contributes to sustainable fashion by giving pre-loved products a new lease on life. Shopping at AVC is not just a transaction; it's a commitment to style, uniqueness, and eco-conscious choices.
